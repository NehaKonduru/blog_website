EXPERIENCE THE BEAUTY, ART, CULTURE AND HISTORY OF ITALY

Author - Rem Malloy
27 January. 6 min read

Italy is a country with something for everyone. The art and statues of Florence, the fountains and sights of ancient Rome and the Vatican, the food of Bologna. The shopping of Milan, wine of Tuscany, ruins of Pompeii and the amazing coastlines. Beautiful lakes, stunning mountains and all things in between. Whether you are on a food and wine journey or are seeking to learn more about the history and archeology, Italy has it all.

Vatican City is the smallest country in the world and holds an unrivalled wealth of art and architectural masterpieces. There is no other place quite like it in the world. Take in the grandeur of St. Peter’s Basilica and explore the secret archives and the opulent Palazzo and Papal Apartments. Visit the collections at the Vatican Museums, including the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel and the many masterpieces of Michelangelo. Admire the history of Castel Sant’Angelo, the largest fortress in Europe. See the intriguing necropolis, or catacombs, that stretch for miles beneath the city. Take a stroll around the Vatican gardens and soak up the tranquility and natural beauty of this magical place.
Cinque Terre is a stunning group of five fishing villages that lie atop the southwestern Italian Riviera. Perched on the edge of the Mediterranean Sea between the mountains and the sea, this picturesque location is a must visit destination. With its vibrant atmosphere, breathtaking scenery and hidden inlets, Cinque Terre offers a unique experience.

Milan is a breathtaking, cosmopolitan city. From world-class art and fashion to a rich and vibrant cultural heritage, Milan offers a one-of-a-kind experience. Explore the iconic Duomo di Milano, the Piazza del Duomo and the La Scala opera house. Each offers a unique and unforgettable experience. Relax and unwind in one of the many city parks. Enjoy a delicious Italian meal at a local trattoria or shop for the latest designer trends.

The Amalfi Coast is an iconic Italian destination known for its beautiful sea-cliffs, picturesque villages, and magnificent views of the Mediterranean. A starkly beautiful coastline, the Amalfi Coast has something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway or an unforgettable adventure, you’ll find that, and more! Take a boat tour of the stunning coast and experience the rustic fishing villages and hamlets dotting the dramatic landscape. Explore the cobblestoned streets of picturesque cities like Positano, Sorrento, and Ravello. Discover the ruins of ancient Pompeii, one of the world’s most iconic archaeological sites, and experience the legacy of the once-mighty Roman Empire. Journey through the heart of this ancient city, where every corner reveals a story 2,000 years in the making. Explore the pathways of time and explore Pompeii’s incredible ruins, and witness the famous eruption of Mt. Vesuvius that destroyed the city in 79 AD. You will be able to see the remains of the vibrant city life, including the unique frescoes, temples, baths, piers, and amphitheaters, giving you a glimpse into the flourishing city once was. From the theatre to the forum, this archaeological wonder will take your breath away and will provide you a glimpse into what life was like in the days of the Roman Empire. Come and see Pompeii – an unforgettable spectacle and experience of a lifetime!
